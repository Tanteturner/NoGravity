Orb Tags:
#retreating
- makes the ball call the orb/retrieve function

#retreat_on_ground
- adds itself the "retreating" tag when {inGround:1b}

#retreat_on_hit
- adds itself the "retreating" tag when hitting an enemy

#destroy_foliage
- destroys all blocks in foliage tag when passing through

#destroy_glass
- destroys glass and glass pane blocks
